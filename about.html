Crafted for the way you live

Furniro is a modern furniture studio designing pieces that last. From dining tables carved out of solid oak to bedroom sets built for calm, every product is made from responsibly sourced materials and finished by hand.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Furniro started as a small workshop of three friends who believed furniture should be beautiful, honest and built to last a lifetime.

Today we ship worldwide while keeping every piece hand-finished in our studio.
